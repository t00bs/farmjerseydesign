gavelEligibility conditions · RSS Guidance 2026
  • Market Garden Area earns 35 cr/vg on up to 5 vergées, and requires at least 5 different crops per vergée.
  • Organic incentives apply only to certified organic parcels (Tier 1 certification).
  • Conservation Crop must be sown purely for environmental benefit — excludes grazing and anything seeded before 01/01/25.
  • Regenerative Practice & Buffer Strips apply to cultivable land only; buffer strips ≤6 m wide, identified on a Conservation & Landscape Plan.
  • Cover crops must follow a primary crop; soil cover crops must not be harvested and removed.
  • Choosing an option is your assertion that the field qualifies under the guidance.
